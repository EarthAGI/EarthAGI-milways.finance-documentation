<Info>
To access the Milways.Finance platform more quickly and conveniently, you can add it as an app icon on your phone's home screen. This way, you can open Milways.Finance directly by tapping the icon, without having to enter the URL through a browser each time. Here are the steps for common mobile operating systems.
</Info>
