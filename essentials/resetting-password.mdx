Description: 'A step-by-step guide to reset your Milways.Finance password if you have forgotten it.'
---

<Info>
Forgot your password? No problem. This guide will walk you through the process of resetting your password securely. Follow these simple steps to regain access to your account.
</Info>

## Password Reset Steps

![Forgot Password button](https://content.gitbook.com/content/zdWZaOjYJalDOu3og601/blobs/cAKdkb5ZpnjmoYN65hGt/94.png)

1. 🔐 On the login page, click the "Forgot Password?" link.

![Enter email and submit](https://content.gitbook.com/content/zdWZaOjYJalDOu3og601/blobs/FeOqvQzYITDJTE82DUiFX/95.png)

2. 📝 Enter the email address associated with your account.
3. 📲 Click the "Submit" button to proceed.

![Password reset email](https://content.gitbook.com/content/zdWZaOjYJalDOu3og601/blobs/mtZznu4hhqfNkeaJs5cj/96.png)

4. 📧 Check your inbox for an email with the subject "Password Reset" and click the "Reset Password" button inside.

![Set new password](https://content.gitbook.com/content/zdWZaOjYJalDOu3og601/blobs/uJy8yCiltoLLcqSc0kSY/97.png)

5. 🔐 Set a new, strong password for your account.
6. 🔄 Confirm the new password by entering it again and click "Reset Password".

![Password reset success](https://content.gitbook.com/content/zdWZaOjYJalDOu3og601/blobs/m9MLZdy2B2o6OwxqCWZI/98.png)

7. 🎉 Success! Your password has been changed.

<Card title="Login with Your New Password" icon="key" href="https://pro.milways.finance/login">
  Your password has been successfully reset. You can now log in to your account using your new password.
</Card>
