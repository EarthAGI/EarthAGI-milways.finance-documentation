<Info>
Milways.Finance platform enables quick and secure wallet transfers between users. Whether you're paying a friend or moving funds, you can easily complete the transaction using Milways.Finance UID. This guide will walk you through the transfer process.
</Info>

## Initiate Transfer

## Enter Transfer Details

![Transferring Credit 2](https://www.gitbook.com/cdn-cgi/image/dpr=2,width=760,onerror=redirect,format=auto/https%3A%2F%2Fcontent.gitbook.com%2Fcontent%2FYsrj5O8moOrzPyGksbuI%2Fblobs%2FHoRUZqJrgdyuAwFEUoQU%2F53.png&width=400&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=e40a5029&sv=2)

3. üí± **Select Currency**: From the dropdown menu, choose which currency you want to transfer.
4. üÜî **Enter Recipient's UID**: Enter the recipient's Milways.Finance UID in the designated field.

<Warning>Please carefully verify the recipient's Milways.Finance UID. Entering an incorrect UID may result in funds being transferred to the wrong account and cannot be recovered.</Warning>

![Transferring Credit 3](https://www.gitbook.com/cdn-cgi/image/dpr=2,width=760,onerror=redirect,format=auto/https%3A%2F%2Fcontent.gitbook.com%2Fcontent%2FYsrj5O8moOrzPyGksbuI%2Fblobs%2FGDc4lBYGrMcwXsd06gZK%2F54.png&width=400&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=a95ae160&sv=2)

5. üî¢ **Enter Amount**: Enter the amount you wish to transfer in the "Enter Value" field.
6. üîê **Security Verification**: Enter your 6-digit authentication code in the field provided. If you haven't set up 2FA yet, please refer to the [Setting Up 2FA Security Code](set2fa.mdx) guide.

## Complete Transfer

![Transferring Credit 4](https://www.gitbook.com/cdn-cgi/image/dpr=2,width=760,onerror=redirect,format=auto/https%3A%2F%2Fcontent.gitbook.com%2Fcontent%2FYsrj5O8moOrzPyGksbuI%2Fblobs%2FUGLTsaSe1BC7dmrdFuLh%2F55.png&width=400&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=d916c3c0&sv=2)

7. ‚úÖ **Execute Transfer**: After confirming all information (recipient UID, currency, amount) is correct, tap the **"Transfer"** button at the bottom of the screen.

<Tip>UID transfers within the Milways.Finance platform are typically instant and free of charge, making them very convenient and efficient.</Tip>
