<Info>
Milways.Finance platform enables quick and secure wallet transfers between users. Whether you're paying a friend or moving funds, you can easily complete the transaction using Milways.Finance UID. This guide will walk you through the transfer process.
</Info>

## Initiate Transfer

## Enter Transfer Details

![Transferring Credit 2](https://files.gitbook.com/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FBdfmzoYxBtV1Xo4FcaHt%2Fuploads%2FchsUy7kPBHWe3dLF4WAC%2F51.png?alt=media&token=19656783-ea28-43e1-b4dc-d556db5e44fe)

3. üí± **Select Currency**: From the dropdown menu, choose which currency you want to transfer.

<Warning>Please carefully verify the recipient's Milways.Finance UID. Entering an incorrect UID may result in funds being transferred to the wrong account and cannot be recovered.</Warning>

![Transferring Credit 3](https://files.gitbook.com/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FBdfmzoYxBtV1Xo4FcaHt%2Fuploads%2FA3gKrRzXSWm4FU9gDhWG%2F52.png?alt=media&token=9eccf73c-61ad-4b24-9ebf-8ebc2c41e547)

## Complete Transfer

![Transferring Credit 4](https://files.gitbook.com/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FBdfmzoYxBtV1Xo4FcaHt%2Fuploads%2F7Lfgol8IEkdKPVr2lwpr%2F53.png?alt=media&token=5be60348-2e44-4a89-a13a-667c80df12c9)

<Tip>UID transfers within the Milways.Finance platform are typically instant and free of charge, making them very convenient and efficient.</Tip>
