<Info>
Milways.Finance platform enables quick and secure wallet transfers between users. Whether you're paying a friend or moving funds, you can easily complete the transaction using Milways.Finance UID. This guide will walk you through the transfer process.
</Info>

## Enter Transfer Details

![Transferring Credit 2](https://open.gitbook.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F356381455-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FB1yREUnIgMhqawNhajWe%252Fuploads%252F2yFqAGh3KYOhN9TEwI8B%252F53.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D0bca7e06-5949-40b5-9432-8f79323dcd5e&width=400&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=e40a5029&sv=2)

3. üí± **Select Currency**: From the dropdown menu, choose which currency you want to transfer.
4. üÜî **Enter Recipient's UID**: Enter the recipient's Milways.Finance UID in the designated field.

<Warning>Please carefully verify the recipient's Milways.Finance UID. Entering an incorrect UID may result in funds being transferred to the wrong account and cannot be recovered.</Warning>

![Transferring Credit 3](https://open.gitbook.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F356381455-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FB1yREUnIgMhqawNhajWe%252Fuploads%252FvlWPZVpk2lBVu9H90DXo%252F54.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D9c7af276-25a2-4c7c-b5ea-065bb733929b&width=400&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=a95ae160&sv=2)

5. üî¢ **Enter Amount**: Enter the amount you wish to transfer in the "Enter Value" field.

## Complete Transfer
