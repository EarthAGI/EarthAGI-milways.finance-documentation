<Info>
After adding your Milways.Finance to Google Wallet, you can use your phone for convenient and secure contactless payments at stores and apps that support Google Pay. This guide will walk you through the steps of adding your card.
</Info>

<Tip>Make sure your Android device supports NFC and has the latest version of the Google Wallet app installed.</Tip>

## Getting Started

![Adding Card to Google Wallet 1](https://files.gitbook.com/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FBdfmzoYxBtV1Xo4FcaHt%2Fuploads%2F5Mfug29n4K5vx7a4hoFX%2F66.png?alt=media&token=4f4b99cf-a883-446d-86dd-3efce6f607aa)

1. üì≤ **Open Google Wallet**: Find and open the Google Wallet app on your Android phone.

![Adding Card to Google Wallet 2](https://files.gitbook.com/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FBdfmzoYxBtV1Xo4FcaHt%2Fuploads%2FSFnr8vzih6196NuxJuBO%2F67.png?alt=media&token=dcd6a524-823d-44e0-8e73-6baed373f6d0)

2. ‚ûï **Add a Card**: On the Google Wallet main screen, tap **"Add payment card"** or a similar button (usually with a "+" sign).

## Enter Card Information

![Adding Card to Google Wallet 3](https://files.gitbook.com/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FBdfmzoYxBtV1Xo4FcaHt%2Fuploads%2FeuMd4UqQaHbZxJn24fIE%2F68.png?alt=media&token=e716dae9-4719-4c44-aca2-ec2acdd86ab0)

3. üí≥ **Select Card Type**: Choose **"New credit or debit card"**.
4. üì∏ **Enter Card Details**: You can either use your phone's camera to scan your physical Milways.Finance to automatically read the card number and expiry date, or tap **"Enter details manually"** to input the card number, expiry date, security code (CVV), cardholder name, and billing address manually.
5. üíæ **Save Information**: After filling in and verifying all details, tap **"Save and continue"** at the bottom of the screen.

## Accept Terms and Verify

![Adding Card to Google Wallet 4](https://files.gitbook.com/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FBdfmzoYxBtV1Xo4FcaHt%2Fuploads%2FeuMd4UqQaHbZxJn24fIE%2F68.png?alt=media&token=e716dae9-4719-4c44-aca2-ec2acdd86ab0)

6. üìÑ **Read Terms**: Google Wallet will display the issuer's (Milways.Finance) terms of service. Please read them carefully.
7. ‚úÖ **Accept Terms**: After reading, tap **"Accept"** to continue.

![Adding Card to Google Wallet 5](https://files.gitbook.com/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FBdfmzoYxBtV1Xo4FcaHt%2Fuploads%2Foii1W4yqA4cMwtsHPMLm%2F69.png?alt=media&token=01729d75-4cd2-4821-88b9-5428e83f8390)

8. üîê **Choose Verification Method**: To confirm you're the legitimate cardholder, you'll need to verify your card. You can typically choose to receive a verification code via SMS, email, or verify through your bank/issuer's app. Select a verification method from the list and press **"Continue"**.

9. üîë **Enter Verification Code**: If you chose to receive a code via SMS or email, check for the code and enter it in the Google Wallet app, then press **"Continue"**. If verification was completed through another app, you may not need to enter a code.

## Completion

After successful verification, your Milways.Finance will be added to Google Wallet. You can now use your phone for payments wherever Google Pay is accepted!

<Tip>You can manage your cards, set default payment cards, and view transaction history in the Google Wallet app.</Tip>
