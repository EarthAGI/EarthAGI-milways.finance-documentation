<Info>
If you have received a Milways.Finance physical card with an activation code, this guide will walk you through the process of activating it in the application.
</Info>

<Tip>Please note: You must have your physical card and the provided activation code on hand to complete this process.</Tip>

## Starting the Activation Process

![Activating Card 1](https://milways.gitbook.io/spidercard/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F905664024-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FBdfmzoYxBtV1Xo4FcaHt%252Fuploads%252F0yNOCOJG3dMobqakil3g%252F106.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D4158755f-b2a2-4f17-889b-eb7594f8bf8f&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=c12560bb&sv=2)

1. üè¶ **Access the Credit Card Section**: Click the "Credit Card" option at the top of the page.
2. üÜï **Activate the Card**: On the new page, click the "Activate Card" button to begin the activation process.

![Activating Card 2](https://milways.gitbook.io/spidercard/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F905664024-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FBdfmzoYxBtV1Xo4FcaHt%252Fuploads%252Fwx9LjeKzdaE8ImPod6rL%252F107.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D61c74a54-e9c0-4dac-86d7-09197d3cb706&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=d0416954&sv=2)

3. üíµ **Select Currency**: In the currency selection interface, choose your preferred currency from the drop-down menu.
4. üéÅ **Enter Discount Code** (Optional): If you have a discount code, enter it in the designated field. For example, a code might provide a 32 USDT discount.

![Activating Card 3](https://milways.gitbook.io/spidercard/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F905664024-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FBdfmzoYxBtV1Xo4FcaHt%252Fuploads%252F5qP9G6z5jXC89b3xzwxS%252F108.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D967c076f-26ef-499b-ab04-22d64fc1d779&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=a129a3ff&sv=2)

5. üè† **Enter Card Shipping Address**: Enter the complete and accurate mailing address in the address field.
6. ‚úÖ **Confirm Card Receipt Status**: Check this box to confirm that you have already received your physical card.

<Warning>Please ensure you provide an accurate mailing address if you have not yet received the card. If you have received it, confirm the details match.</Warning>

![Activating Card 4](https://milways.gitbook.io/spidercard/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F905664024-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FBdfmzoYxBtV1Xo4FcaHt%252Fuploads%252Fs03rSwzWoEVbYFFZNX9N%252F109.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D8fa4ff84-ddc6-46db-925f-d34f82db47c2&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=78310bc9&sv=2)

7. üì≤ **Enter Customer Number**: Input your 16-digit customer number, which can be found on your physical card, into the "Customer Number" field.
8. üîë **Enter Activation Code**: Input the unique activation code that was provided with your physical card.

![Activating Card 5](https://milways.gitbook.io/spidercard/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F905664024-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FBdfmzoYxBtV1Xo4FcaHt%252Fuploads%252FUtixwKK71sonavfATLjh%252F110.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D3a62ec38-eb8d-43e9-91f4-b670eb1be07a&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=cba3b1b&sv=2)

9. ‚úÖ **Complete Activation**: Tap the "Pay and Activate" button to finalize your card activation.

<Tip>
* The application fee is 200 USDT.
* The system supports multiple currencies, including USDT and HKD.
* After activation, the interface may show an available credit limit of HK$0.00 until funds are loaded.
* It is recommended to double-check all entered information to ensure successful activation.
</Tip>
