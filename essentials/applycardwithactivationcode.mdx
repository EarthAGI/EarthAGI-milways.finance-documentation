<Info>
If you have received a Milways.Finance physical card with an activation code, this guide will walk you through the process of activating it in the application.
</Info>

<Tip>Please note: You must have your physical card and the provided activation code on hand to complete this process.</Tip>

## Starting the Activation Process

![Activating Card 1](https://open-2c.gitbook.com/url/preview/site_CM7n1/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fcontent.gitbook.com%2Fcontent%2FUws1EHx12NSC5Ao99NOb%2Fblobs%2FsZs6tjmCOYBeav4duYIO%2F106.png&width=768&dpr=1&quality=100&sign=1f8e458b&sv=2)

![Activating Card 2](https://open-2c.gitbook.com/url/preview/site_CM7n1/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fcontent.gitbook.com%2Fcontent%2FUws1EHx12NSC5Ao99NOb%2Fblobs%2Fsjr2jq7cBA7VvNXfBGUf%2F107.png&width=768&dpr=1&quality=100&sign=e0ad3b12&sv=2)

5. üè† **Enter Card Shipping Address**: Enter the complete and accurate mailing address in the address field.

<Warning>Please ensure you provide an accurate mailing address if you have not yet received the card. If you have received it, confirm the details match.</Warning>

![Activating Card 4](https://open-2c.gitbook.com/url/preview/site_CM7n1/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F4257403346-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FUws1EHx12NSC5Ao99NOb%252Fuploads%252F4V2qolDXLIQU6Cb6Tovi%252F109.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D94d56f19-be77-4240-a2ca-aab792d6c5f0&width=768&dpr=1&quality=100&sign=83261c25&sv=2)

11. ‚úÖ **Complete Activation**: Tap the "Pay and Activate" button to finalize your card activation.

<Tip>
* The application fee is 200 USDT.
* The system supports multiple currencies, including USDT and HKD.
* After activation, the interface may show an available credit limit of HK$0.00 until funds are loaded.
* It is recommended to double-check all entered information to ensure successful activation.
</Tip>
