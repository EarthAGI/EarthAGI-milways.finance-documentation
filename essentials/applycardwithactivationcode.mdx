<Info>
If you have received a physical card or an activation code, this guide will walk you through the process of activating your Milways.Finance card.
</Info>

<Tip>Please note: Activating a card with a code may be part of a specific promotion or distribution channel. Ensure your code is valid and has not expired.</Tip>

## Starting the Activation Process

<Tip>
* The application fee is 200 USDT.
* The interface will show an available credit limit of HK$0.00.
* The system supports multiple currencies, including USDT and HKD.
* It is recommended to double-check all entered information to ensure successful activation.
</Tip>
